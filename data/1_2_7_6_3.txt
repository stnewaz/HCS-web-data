Fearless Journeys: Thriving On Campus. We are dedicated to exploring the world of mental health and the impact on college students. Our mission is to empower and educate listeners by looking at the differing factors that influence mental well-being. Through engaging discussions, expert insights, and thought-provoking interviews, we aim to provide students with knowledge, strategies, and support they need to navigate their college years.
Published bi-weekly.       
      
Hosted by Steve Johnson and Corrine Janzen.

Seasons published Sep-Dec and Jan-Apr

What if your next adventure could help you build confidence, manage stress, and strengthen your mental health? In this episode of Fearless Journeys: Thriving On Campus, we dive into the world of Dungeons & Dragons with David Lindskoog, a Registered Clinical Counsellor, to explore how role-playing games can be more than just fun—they can be transformative. Whether you’re a seasoned player or have never rolled a d20 in your life, this episode will show you how storytelling, teamwork, and imagination can help you level up in real life. Tune in and discover how D&D might just be the mental health boost you never knew you needed!
