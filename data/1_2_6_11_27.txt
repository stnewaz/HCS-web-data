Your Choice, Our Support is a comic produced by the SVSPO to help the SFU and FIC community understand how to access support services offered by the SVSPO. Our primary goal was to raise awareness about how to access "anonymous disclosure" options through our office.

We hope that by reading the comic, you realize that you are not alone, help is available, and the SVSPO team is here to support you in ways that feel right for you. 

Are you interested in learning more about anonymous disclosures? If so, please read our blog post!
