Mindfulness Meditation is a free drop-in class for SFU students, faculty, and staff. Monday sessions are geared towards self-compassion, and Wednesday sessions are regular mindfulness practices.

No previous experience is necessary.

Each session includes mindfulness practice, information on habits of mind, and the opportunity for a brief discussion about the practice. 

The sessions are drop-ins - no registration required.

If you prefer to practice in person, join us on the Vancouver campus for our weekly Breathwork and Meditation practice. 

 

A variety of HCS staffers facilitate these drop-in sessions, please email Sarah Parkes at sacomin@sfu.ca for more information.
