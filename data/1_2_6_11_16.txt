Are you ready to make a difference? In this self-paced online module, you’ll explore the realities and impact of sexualized violence—and examine the factors that contribute to its presence in our communities. Through interactive content and real-world scenarios, you’ll learn how to recognize harmful behaviors, understand the vital role of consent in all relationships, and gain practical tools to safely intervene when needed.

Whether you're looking to support a friend, empower yourself, or become a proactive ally, this course will equip you with the knowledge and confidence to take meaningful action. You’ll also learn where to find support and how to be there for others who have been affected by sexualized violence. 

This module is available through Canvas, SFU's online learning platform. The estimated time to complete this module is 60-75 minutes. 

Enroll for Safer Campuses for Everyone

Safer Campuses for Everyone is recognized on the Co-Curricular Record (CCR), an official university document that tracks students' co-curricular involvement at SFU. To receive 1.5 hours of CCR recognition, make sure you complete the final learning reflection in the module. Safer Campuses will show up on your CCR in the term after you complete the module (eg. if you complete the module in September, it will show up on your CCR in the Spring Term).
