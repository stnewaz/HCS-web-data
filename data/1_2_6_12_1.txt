In a partnership between CAL and Health and Counselling, individual and group counselling services are available to CAL registrants. The services are being provided by the CAL counsellor, Sean Heaslip, a registered clinical counsellor who is also partially blind, and has personal experience navigating both academic and broader social contexts with a disability.  

Experiencing Disability Group

The first round of the Experiencing disability group begins Thursday, January 14th, 2021, so be sure to express interest to your CAL advisor by email so you can set up an intake appointment with Sean to participate.

Individual Counselling

Intake appointments for CAL registrants seeking individual counselling with Sean are also available starting this week.

The referral process for participating in either individual or group counselling through this CAL pilot are the same.

Services will be provided in a remote format (phone/video conference) for the 2021 spring semester. This project is intended to provide fast track access for CAL registrants to short term supportive individual and group counselling services in partnership with SFU Health and Counselling Services (HCS).   

We welcome ensuring equitable access to this service. Please let Sean know of any access related needs as soon as possible.  

Should you have further questions feel free to reach out to your CAL advisor, or contact Sean directly at sean_heaslip@sfu.ca
