Join us for weekly breathwork and meditation sessions. These drop-ins are open to students, faculty, and staff, and provide a valuable opportunity to pause, recharge, and reconnect.

Designed to support calmness, clarity, and emotional balance, each session includes guided meditation, breathwork, and a take-home practice to deepen your awareness beyond class. Whether you're new to mindfulness or already have experience, you'll find a welcoming and supportive space to manage stress and cultivate inner resilience.

There's no need to register. The sessions are drop-ins, so just show up.

Can’t make it to the in-person sessions in Vancouver? Join our weekly online Mindfulness Meditation practice and enjoy the same guided experience from anywhere.
