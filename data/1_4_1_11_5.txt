SurveyMonkey is a intuitive full-featured web survey tool with a wide variety of question types and reporting. This service is provided to support surveys for university-related teaching, learning, research, and administrative purposes. The SurveyMonkey application and data are hosted in Canada by a commercial provider external to SFU.

All support for the SurveyMonkey Service, including end-user and survey support, is provided by SurveyMonkey. This includes product feature capabilities, survey creation, technical issues, and support for survey respondents. Survey support can be found at SurveyMonkey's help site.

SFU IT Services can help with account authorization and access through the CAS login page.
