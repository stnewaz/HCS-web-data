Your time at university is valuable for learning and personal growth. It's normal to feel stressed or tired during this journey. Prioritizing your well-being and supporting others can help you stay on track to achieve your goals. Check out our available services and resources, and seek support when needed.

If you're feeling overwhelmed and need support, or if you are supporting a friend or student in crisis, there are options.

Health & Counselling has various mental health professionals available for individual support including registered clinical counsellors, psychologists, psychiatrists, social workers, and mental health nurses. 

Explore your identity and find a sense of community with options that connect you to folks experiencing similar journeys. Explore what's available on and off campus.

Explore options at your own pace like courses and trainings, videos, podcasts, and apps and build skills related to relevant topics like procrastination, mental health, well-being, and resilience.
