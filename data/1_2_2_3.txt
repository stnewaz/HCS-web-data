I haven’t seen a review posted for the app so I figured I’d give my two cents. If you use this app, I do not recommend the chat function at all. The counselor I spoke to was so dry they could’ve been a robot. I was legit telling them I’m having mental health issues due to feeling like I’m not important or ignored and they take minutes to respond and simply suggest to seek a psychiatrist. No “I’m sorry you feel that way” or “that must be hard for you”. I’ve dealt with customer service bots more empathetic than this counselor I had last night. I have been connected with a licensed psychologist and seen them over the course of a semester completely for free, and I think that is the strength of this app. The chat function was genuinely so horrible I’m very glad I didn’t take it too personally and left the chat when I felt like the counselor wasn’t doing anything helpful. I left feedback on the app itself, but this seems to be a continuing issue based on previous reviews. I think some can find help with this, but I repeat, THE CHAT FUNCTION IS GARBAGE. There is no way to tell if you are speaking with a legitimate counselor or not.
