Support Black Causes and Businesses in Greater Vancouver

What is this Document?



This is a list of causes, organisations, businesses, individuals and services you can support in the greater Vancouver area that are run, owned, co-owned or otherwise benefit the local Black community.





Accuracy, Updates and Additions



While the Information is considered to be true and correct at the date of publication, changes in circumstances after the time of publication may impact on the accuracy of the Information.



This document will be updated periodically based on the capacity of the author and the availability of new information.





How do I Search this Document?



PC: Click on ‘More’ (three dotted horizontal line┆)  in the top, right corner of your browser and the select ‘Find…’ in the context menu that opens. Next, type in your search term. Alternatively you use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + F to open the search function.



iOS: Tap on the share button (it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up). Scroll to the right in the bottom menu and tap "Find on Page." Type in your search word.



Android: Tap ‘More’ (three dotted horizontal line┆) in the top, right corner of your browser. Tap on ‘Find in page’. Type in your search term.





I don’t see my cause or organisation listed; how can I be added?



The next update of this document will contain a link to a submission form allowing you to add your causes, organisations, businesses, individuals and services. Do not message the author with requests for updates, unless you want your listing removed from the current version of the document.
