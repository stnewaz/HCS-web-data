The Safe Walk program is here to ensure that you can navigate campus safely, whether it’s day or night. If you need support, a member of our Campus Public Safety or student safety team is ready to assist you.

Our Safe Walk team is always equipped with radios and flashlights, staying connected to our Security Operations Centre through dispatch. You can easily identify them by their SFU Campus Public Safety ID badges.

We're here to help you feel secure on campus, 24/7!

To request a Safe Walk:

You can request a Safe Walk to anywhere on SFU property.
