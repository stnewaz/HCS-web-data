Paola Ardiles supports civic engagement by having students work in collaboration with partner organizations like Surrey CityLab through service learning.

More infomation on Surrey CityLab

As part of a Teaching and Learning Development Grant in 2019, Surrey CityLab was an experiential program for post-secondary students to address important city topics. Students had the opportunity to engage in real-world issues affecting the city, gained a greater awareness of city priorities and challenges, and became more civic-minded.
