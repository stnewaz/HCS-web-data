The purpose of the Organizational Health and Well-Being office is to help create safe, healthy, inclusive and engaging work environments and provide meaningful opportunities and accommodations for staff and faculty with ongoing illness and disabilities. Our goal is to help employees stay at work and when illness necessitates absences, integrate them back into the work environment safely, expediently, and respectfully
