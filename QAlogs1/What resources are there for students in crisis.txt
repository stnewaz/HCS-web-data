Question: What resources are available for supporting students in distress?
Answer: - My SSP (Student Support Program) offers free, confidential 24/7 mental health support.
- Access My SSP through the app or by calling 1.844.451.9700 (or 001.416.380.6578 outside North America).
- Promote My SSP and Health and Counselling Services.
- Download the My SSP app and encourage students to do the same.
- Assist students in accessing My SSP with their consent.
- Assisted Referral: help students connect with My SSP for the first time.
- Outreach Request: ask My SSP to call the student.
- Call 1.844.451.9700 for staff/faculty consultation on supporting students.
- Chat with an advisor through the My SSP app for advice.
- Contact SFU Health and Counselling Services during office hours for assistance.
- Learn how to support students in distress and identify signs of distress.
- Resources and support services for SFU faculty and staff.

Question: Are there any resources available for those in crisis?
Answer: Yes, there are resources available for those in crisis, including the 9-8-8: Suicide Crisis Helpline, which offers a safe space to talk 24 hours a day, every day of the year. If safety is at risk, individuals are advised to call 9-1-1 immediately.
