2.
The purpose of the group-generated study guide assignment is to encourage students to work effectively in teams while building professional skills such as note taking, group work, and study skills. It helps students integrate course information and prepares them for exams.

3.
Students collaborate by working in groups of four or five to develop a one-page study guide on a specific course topic. They create and submit a group contract outlining how they will work together and post their guides on Canvas for other students to use in exam preparation. They are graded on both the quality of their teamwork and the final product.
