Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT) teaches evidence-based techniques to improve emotional regulation, interpersonal effectiveness, distress tolerance, and mindfulness. It provides a supportive environment to learn practical skills for managing intense emotions, coping with stress, and building healthier relationships.
