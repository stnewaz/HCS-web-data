2.
The purpose of the informational interview assignment created by Tara Immell is to give students an opportunity to learn about potential job opportunities and develop professional contacts that will help them navigate future job uncertainties.

3.
Students are asked to conduct an informational interview with someone whose position relates to the class content. They can choose a person in a company they have an interest in joining or someone holding a job they’d like to have. They can conduct the interview alone or with a partner and are required to prepare a 6-8 minute presentation (either live or pre-recorded) about their experience. Instructions are provided on how to approach someone to request an interview and how to follow up after the interview.
