The purpose of the #dearsurvivorsfu project is to offer a message of hope, care, and support to people who have experienced sexual violence, acknowledging their unique journeys and emphasizing that they are seen, believed, and supported. It aims to validate their experiences without trivializing the realities of trauma and recovery.
