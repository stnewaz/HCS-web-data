1.
Your benefit covers counselling services from a qualified mental health provider, including psychologists, clinical counsellors, and social workers.

2.
1. Tell your provider that you have coverage through the Health Benefits Program when scheduling your first appointment.
2. Your first appointment is about determining if the provider is the right fit for you; you can change to another registered provider at any time.
3. Cancel your appointment ahead of time if you can no longer make it, as missed appointments are not covered. 
4. For detailed information about your mental health claims, access your PBC Member Profile online.
5. If you need travel assistance to access counselling, contact the Medical Transportation Program (email: Transportation@fnha.ca; phone: 1-855-550-5454).
6. If you prefer virtual appointments, talk to your provider about Telehealth options.

3.
