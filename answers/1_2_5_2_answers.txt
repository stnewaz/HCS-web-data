You can get support as a new international student by contacting the International Student Services (ISS) at Simon Fraser University (SFU). Additionally, you can follow the SFU Global Instagram and Student Central Instagram for updates and resources.

3.

4.
The global community at Simon Fraser University (SFU) is diverse and inclusive, encompassing international students from various backgrounds. It offers resources and support for new international students, promotes study abroad opportunities, and fosters connections through platforms like the SFU Global Instagram and the Student Central Instagram. The community is enriched by an acknowledgment of the traditional territories of several Indigenous peoples, highlighting SFU's commitment to inclusivity and respect for cultural heritage.
