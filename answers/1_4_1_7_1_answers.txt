10.
An inclusive learning environment demonstrates intentional consideration for all students, enhancing positive well-being. It makes students feel safer, more welcomed, and ensures the learning environment is accessible for everyone. Inclusivity allows all students to be recognized and respected, making them feel included in the classroom space.

11.
Opportunities for personal and professional growth can increase students’ skills, resiliency, and preparedness for the future. By engaging in growth experiences, students build confidence and enhance their ability to navigate challenges, fostering resilience.

12.
Flexibility in learning experiences is important for student empowerment because it helps students feel empowered and supported. It allows them to have control over their learning, accommodates personal struggles that may interfere with meeting assignment expectations, and enables them to contribute to the structure and content of their classes based on current events or their needs and interests.
