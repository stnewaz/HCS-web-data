The main goals of the Bouncing Forward training are to help individuals build personal resilience, identify and access resources, and improve their well-being. Participants will learn to describe how resilience aids in flexibility and adaptability to change, examine the relationship between stress, success, well-being, and resilience, and practice resilience-building strategies.
