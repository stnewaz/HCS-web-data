A "404 Not Found" error can negatively impact website SEO by:

1. Reducing user experience, leading to higher bounce rates.
2. Causing search engines to devalue the website if errors are frequent.
3. Hindering the indexing of pages if important content is not accessible.
4. Leading to loss of potential backlinks if the linked pages are not found.
5. Affecting the overall authority and trustworthiness of the site in search engine rankings.
