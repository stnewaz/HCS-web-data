Do's:
- Share personal stories that connect emotionally with the audience.
- Use evidence-based information to support your narrative.
- Tailor your message to resonate with the values of your audience.
- Encourage dialogue and active listening during discussions.

Don'ts:
- Avoid using alarmist language that may induce fear without offering solutions.
- Steer clear of overwhelming your audience with too much data or technical jargon.
- Don’t dismiss or invalidate the feelings of others regarding climate change.
- Refrain from blaming or shaming individuals for their choices related to climate issues.
