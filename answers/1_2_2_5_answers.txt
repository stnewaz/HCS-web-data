When making international Wi-Fi calls, consider the following:

1. Check if the call to a US number is free while traveling internationally; it usually is.
2. Be aware of different rules for calling international numbers, as carriers have varying policies.
3. Use third-party apps like Skype or WhatsApp when connected to Wi-Fi to avoid issues with your carrier.
4. Avoid using cellular data while roaming to prevent high charges; turn off data roaming in settings before traveling.
