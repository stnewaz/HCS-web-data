8.
The December 11 workshop approaches the topic of singleness by inviting participants to explore the joys and strengths of being single through intentional journaling. It reframes singleness as a time rich with growth, autonomy, and self-discovery. Participants will engage with thoughtful prompts that encourage self-appreciation, challenge cultural narratives about relationships, and celebrate the unique freedom of living for oneself.
