The term "Two-Spirit" in the context of this program signifies a cultural identity among some Indigenous people that encompasses both masculine and feminine spirits, reflecting a unique understanding of gender and sexuality. It highlights the program's inclusivity and recognition of diverse identities within the trans and non-binary community.
