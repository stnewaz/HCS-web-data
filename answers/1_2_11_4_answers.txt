1.
Drug checking is a quick, free, and confidential service that analyzes drug samples to identify their contents. Technicians use technologies like FTIR and test strips to provide results and answer any questions about the sample. A small sample, about the size of half a matchstick head, is taken for analysis, which typically takes five to fifteen minutes. Results are communicated by a technician, and no identifying information is tracked or retained.

2.
Drug checking services are offered across various locations in British Columbia (BC).
