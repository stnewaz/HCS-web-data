The Scarborough Charter on Anti-Black Racism and Black Inclusion in Higher Education is a commitment by institutions across Canada to combat anti-Black racism and foster Black inclusion in higher education. It was co-created through extensive consultations and collaboration with Black communities, academic institutions, and various institutional partners. The Charter recognizes the realities of anti-Black racism and includes concrete steps for action to ensure institutional and cross-sector accountabilities.

The Statement of Acknowledgement of Traditional Land recognizes the historical and ongoing presence of Indigenous peoples on the land where the University of Toronto operates. It honors the traditional custodians of the land, acknowledges their rights, and expresses gratitude for the opportunity to work on that land.
