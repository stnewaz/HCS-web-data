1.
The mission of Trans Student Educational Resources (TSER) is to transform the educational environment for trans and gender non-conforming students through advocacy and empowerment, educate the public, teach trans activists how to be effective organizers, and promote justice for trans and gender nonconforming youth through an intersectional framework of activism.

11.
The significance of an intersectional framework in TSER's activism is that it emphasizes the importance of addressing multiple forms of oppression simultaneously, ensuring that the fight for justice for trans and gender nonconforming youth is inclusive and considers various social identities and experiences. This approach fosters collaborative action, which is essential for achieving long-term change.
