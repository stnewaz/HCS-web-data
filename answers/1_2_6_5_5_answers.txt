The mission of Hogan’s Alley Society (HAS) is to advance the social, political, economic, and cultural well-being of people of African descent in Metro Vancouver through culturally informed housing, nonprofit housing development, and community engagement.

2.
Culturally informed housing, nonprofit housing development, community engagement.

3.
Hogan's Alley Society supports affordable housing in Metro Vancouver by developing Afrocentric, affordable housing through initiatives like the Hogan's Alley Block, which is part of a Community Land Trust aimed at ensuring long-term affordability and preventing displacement.

4.
The Hogan's Alley Block initiative is a project led by the Hogan’s Alley Society focused on developing Afrocentric, affordable housing through one of Canada's few Black-led Community Land Trusts. It aims to serve as a catalyst for enrichment and belonging, ensuring long-term affordability and preventing further displacement of people of African descent in Metro Vancouver.

5.
A Community Land Trust (CLT) is a nonprofit organization that acquires and manages land for the benefit of a community, ensuring long-term affordability and preventing displacement. In relation to the Hogan’s Alley Society (HAS), it is one of Canada’s few Black-led Community Land Trusts that focuses on developing Afrocentric, affordable housing through initiatives like the Hogan's Alley Block.
