3.
To help your students get to know you better, you can try the following methods:

1. Share personal stories and experiences.
2. Use a PowerPoint presentation to introduce yourself at the beginning of the semester.
3. Engage in icebreaker activities that allow students to learn about your interests and background.
4. Encourage students to share their own stories in return.
5. Be approachable and open to questions about yourself.

4.
Positive classroom culture is significant because it fosters a supportive and engaging learning environment, enhances student well-being, encourages collaboration and respect among students, and improves overall academic performance. It helps students feel valued and connected, which can lead to increased motivation and participation in class.
