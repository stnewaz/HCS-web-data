The workplace environment can positively contribute to mental well-being, but it may also contribute to mental illness by being a direct source of stress, depression, and anxiety, or by exacerbating existing issues. If not well managed, workplace-related mental health issues can lead to increased illness, absenteeism, tension and conflicts between colleagues, deteriorating employee performance, and reduced morale.
