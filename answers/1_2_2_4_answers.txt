2.
You can earn Play Points by making purchases on Google Play, including apps, games, and in-app items. You can also earn points by completing specific activities and promotions offered by Google Play.

4.
To redeem a code on Google Play, open the Google Play Store app, tap on the menu icon, select "Redeem," enter the code, and then tap "Redeem" again to confirm.

5.
The refund policy for purchases on Google Play allows users to request a refund within a specific time frame, typically 48 hours for most purchases. After this period, refunds are generally subject to the developer's policy.

7.
Family sharing on Google Play allows you to share eligible apps, games, movies, TV shows, and books with up to five family members. Each family member can have their own Google account while sharing purchases and subscriptions made by the family group. To set it up, one member must create a family group and invite others to join.
