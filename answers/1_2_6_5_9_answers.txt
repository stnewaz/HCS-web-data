The mission of the Black Emotional and Mental Health Collective (BEAM) is to transform and support the well-being and mental health of marginalized communities through training, funding, and resources for an international network of therapists, wellness practitioners, and grassroots wellness initiatives.

2.
BEAM supports mental health for marginalized communities by training community leaders, therapists, and wellness practitioners in healing justice-informed strategies, funding grassroots wellness initiatives, organizing healing justice efforts, creating spaces for collective healing, holding monthly healing circles, and implementing a "train the trainer" model to ensure sustainability and scalability of their work.
