The National Standard for Mental Health and Well-Being for Post-Secondary Students is the first of its kind in the world and consists of a set of flexible, voluntary guidelines designed to help post-secondary institutions support the mental health and well-being of their students. It is adaptable, allowing each institution to determine what is achievable based on their situation, resources, and community context. The Standard is grounded in a commitment to continuous improvement, enabling institutions to set their own priorities for enhancing student mental health and well-being over time.
