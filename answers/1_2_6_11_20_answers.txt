6.
To respond to disclosures of sexual violence, follow these guidelines:

1. Listen actively and without judgment.
2. Believe the person and validate their feelings.
3. Offer support and options for next steps.
4. Respect their privacy and confidentiality.
5. Encourage them to seek professional help or support services.
6. Avoid asking probing questions about the incident.
7. Provide information about their rights and resources available.
