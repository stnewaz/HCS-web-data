The purpose of the comic "Your Choice, Our Support" is to help the SFU and FIC community understand how to access support services offered by the SVSPO and to raise awareness about accessing "anonymous disclosure" options through their office.

3.
To access anonymous disclosure options through the SVSPO, individuals can contact the SVSPO at 778.782.7233 or sv-support@sfu.ca. Additionally, they can read the blog post for more information on anonymous disclosures.

4.
The SVSPO hopes to convey that individuals are not alone, help is available, and the SVSPO team is there to support them in ways that feel right for them.

5.
You can learn more about anonymous disclosures by reading the blog post mentioned in the comic produced by the SVSPO.
