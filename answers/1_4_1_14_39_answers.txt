1.
I use this resource to enhance the connection between course material and real life, and to increase students' sense of engagement with the learning process. Students choose and design their own research questions based on their interests, utilizing survey data from Statistics Canada. The project is structured in parts to allow for assignment spacing, giving students time to receive instructor feedback. This empowers students and keeps them invested in producing valid answers to questions they care about, while also allowing them to learn from and correct mistakes over time. These activities can be adapted to various disciplines and teaching platforms.
