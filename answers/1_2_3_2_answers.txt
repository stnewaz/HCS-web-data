The purpose of Urgent and Primary Care Centres (UPCC) is to provide patient-centred care for individuals with urgent but non-life-threatening injuries and/or illnesses that need to be seen by a doctor or nurse practitioner within 12 to 24 hours. They offer team-based, everyday health care when patients are unable to see their family doctor or nurse practitioner and their condition does not require emergency attention.
