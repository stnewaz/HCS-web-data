MoodMission is designed to provide a tailored list of simple, quick, effective, evidence-based Missions to improve mood and help prevent everyday mood disturbances from developing into mental health issues.

2.
MoodMission helps users improve their mood by providing a tailored list of 5 simple, quick, effective, evidence-based Missions based on their feelings. Completing these Missions earns users rewards in the app, motivating them to take steps towards becoming healthier, happier, and more confident.

3.
The Missions provided by MoodMission are supported by scientific research, including three published papers in academic peer-reviewed journals and a randomized controlled trial (RCT) that demonstrated MoodMission's effectiveness in decreasing depression and increasing mental wellbeing.

This edition brings a practical and inquiry-based approach, with an emphasis on positive education, and includes wellbeing check-ins throughout to assist with classroom learning and the wellbeing of students. It has been produced in partnership with Smiling Mind and MoodMission, providing key information and activities to support students in managing their own wellbeing.
