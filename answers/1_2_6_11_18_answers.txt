The significance of neurodiversity in the Consent Matters campaign lies in the exploration of how to build consent cultures that affirm neurodivergent experiences and uphold everyone's autonomy. The campaign emphasizes shifting traditional consent frameworks, which often center neurotypical ways of communicating, toward more inclusive and flexible approaches that promote equity, safety, and care for all individuals, including those who are neurodivergent.
