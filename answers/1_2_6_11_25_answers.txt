The Active Bystander Network (ABN) at Simon Fraser University is a program that provides students with the opportunity to engage in sexual violence prevention and education. It aims to raise awareness about sexual violence and foster a culture of care and consent on campus. ABN members are trained to recognize and intervene in situations that may lead to sexual violence and educate the campus community on topics such as consent, rape culture, and available resources. The program is open to all SFU and FIC students, emphasizing the inclusion of historically marginalized voices.
