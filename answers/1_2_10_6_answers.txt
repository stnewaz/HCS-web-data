The purpose of the People Plan is to transform the faculty and staff experience at SFU over the next five years (2023-2028) by listening to the needs of the SFU community and supporting the delivery of the university's academic mission.

14.
The People Plan supports faculty and staff at SFU by providing a clear and practical framework that listens to their needs, fostering an environment for optimal performance. It includes six key themes, 23 early actions, and four working groups to guide success, aligning with SFU's existing and future plans and priorities. The plan incorporates feedback from over 500 faculty members and staff, addressing what they liked, what was missing, and their key priorities for the organization and their teams.

15.
SFU received feedback on what people liked, what they felt was missing, what they wanted to be considered in developing the plan, and their key priorities for both the organization and their teams.
