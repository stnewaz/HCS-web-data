1.
My Student Support Program (My SSP) provides free, confidential, 24/7 mental health support for SFU and FIC students. The service is multilingual and available worldwide, offering access to professional counsellors by phone, chat, or through short-term virtual or in-person appointments. It supports overall well-being, offers immediate assistance and short-term counselling, and includes a library of digital mental-health resources.

2.
All SFU and FIC students can access My SSP services.

3.
Yes, My SSP provides free, confidential, 24/7 mental health support.

4.
You can access counselling through My SSP by:

1. Downloading the My SSP app and creating your profile. 
2. Selecting Simon Fraser University to unlock student access.
3. Connecting 24/7 via in-app chat or phone call.
4. Requesting short-term individual counselling either locally or virtually.
5. Clearly stating your needs, such as the preference for in-person or virtual sessions, or a specific counsellor. 
6. Advocating for yourself during the intake process to ensure you are matched with the right support.

5.
My SSP is multilingual and supports multiple languages.

6.

13.
To use the chat feature, you need access to your provider network (data access) or a Wi-Fi connection. The chat function through the free My SSP app works just as it does when accessed from within Canada or the U.S. If you encounter difficulties, check your internet connectivity.

14.
If you encounter difficulties with the chat feature, check your internet connectivity. Some countries may have additional firewall protections that block certain apps. If this happens, consider calling My SSP instead.

15.
To make a call to My SSP while outside of North America, you have two options:

1. Use the phone number 001-416-380-6578.
2. Use the collect call option, which connects you directly with a representative. Be sure to explain that you are a student accessing My SSP services from outside Canada/U.S.

19.
To access short-term counselling sessions with My SSP, you can: 

1. Call My SSP and clearly state your request, such as wanting to book short-term counselling with the same provider for a few sessions, or requesting a counsellor who matches your lived experience.
2. Download the My SSP app, create your profile, and select Simon Fraser University to unlock student access.
3. Request in-person counselling near your home or virtual counselling.
4. Specify if you want a small number of short-term sessions with continuity.
5. Connect 24/7 via in-app chat or phone.
