The goals of the Canadian Health Promoting Campuses Network include promoting health and wellbeing on campuses and guiding institutions in their health promotion efforts based on the Okanagan Charter.

8.
1. Develop a comprehensive health and wellbeing strategy aligned with the Okanagan Charter principles.
2. Engage stakeholders including students, faculty, and staff in the planning and implementation process.
3. Create supportive environments that promote physical, mental, and social health on campus.
4. Offer programs and services that address the diverse health needs of the campus community.
5. Foster partnerships with local health services and organizations to enhance resources available to students.
6. Evaluate and assess the impact of health initiatives to ensure continuous improvement.
